Rat Stories

Follow Rat, a humble orphan boy, as he discovers the world of magic as the apprentice to the wizard Valakar. As Rat unravels the secrets of magic under Valakar’s harsh tutelage, he is forced to confront the grim reality of Valakar’s malevolent plans, forcing him to grapple with his own past and uncertain future. Join Rat and his companions on an unforgettable quest as they confront the shadows of their past and uncover the true meaning of courage, friendship, and sacrifice in a world overshadowed by violence.

I have finished multiple drafts of this book, and it is now in the hands of an editor, I have hopes of getting this thing out soon!
